WELCOME TO DIVY JYOT

Empowering Citizens for Safer Food in Gujarat

Divy Jyot Foundation is dedicated to creating a Gujarat where every citizen has access to safe, authentic, hygienic, and unadulterated food. Food adulteration is a silent health hazard leading to serious health complications, food-borne illnesses, and long-term health risks. Our campaign seeks to address these challenges through awareness, education, and active community participation.
