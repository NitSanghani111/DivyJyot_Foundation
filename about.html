About Us

Protecting Consumers & Food Safety

Divy Jyot Foundation is a non – Profit Organization committed to Protecting Consumers and Promoting Food safety awareness throughout Gujarat.

"The Foundation Believes that safe food is a basic right and that every citizen deserves protections from adulterated and unsafe food Products."

Through education, advocacy and Community engagement, we work to create awareness about food safety and empower consumer to make informed choices.

Core Purpose

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

A safe, healthy and adulteration-free Gujarat.

We dedicate our efforts towards eradicating toxic adulterants and food fraud, ensuring every household receives quality and safe sustenance.

Our Vision

Access to safe, authentic, hygienic, and unadulterated food.

A future where transparency prevails in the food supply chain and public health is preserved by proactive, educated consumers.
